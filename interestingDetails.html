Interesting Details

  • Wharton had initially begun writing the novel in French, while she was studying in France, however as it became more in depth, it was changed and later published.
  • It is believed that she based the tragic ending to the novel off a tragic sledding accident she had learned about from a friend while working in a library. The friend had been in a sled crash that killed another girl.
  • The author had a very difficult marriage with an older man that was arranged, she eventually divorced her husband; one can draw a lot of similarities from her marriage and the relationship between Ethan and Zeena.
