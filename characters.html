Characters

  • Narrator
  • The narrator is a young engineer living in Ethan's town
  • He witnesses the events of Ethan's life and finds it fascinating enough to tell
  • Ethan Frome
  • Ethan is the protagonist of the novel, he has lived a troubled life.
  • Frome had attended college and had a promising life ahead, or so it seemed, however everything went downhill.
  • Zeena Frome
  • She is Ethan's wife, and is a very demanding
  • Ethan agrees to marry her because she had taken care of his mom when she was aging, however this decision only brings Ethan down
  • Mattie Silver
  • She is Zeena's cousin and the housekeeper at the Frome house
  • Mattie is a pretty young girl, and eventually her and Ethan fall for one another.
  • Their relationship leads to a tragic outcome
